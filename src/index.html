About #DevTO

DevTO is a monthly developer meetup in Toronto, Canada. It's held on the last Monday of every month from 6:30 - 10:00pm.

We encourage and welcome developers of all race and gender, in fact we encourage everyone not just developers to come out to our events.

Sponsorship helps us keep bringing this great event to the community month after month. We love our sponsors and make sure that you get the most bang for your dollars
